Auditable Privacy

Built for Regulatory Standards

Unlike legacy privacy pools that hide transaction traces entirely, Setu introduces a selective disclosure receipt. Receivers can construct a cryptographic witness proving a specific withdrawal was sourced from a valid deposit, without revealing the deposit address.

This allows corporate treasuries and private individuals to meet KYC/AML compliance checks on-chain seamlessly.
